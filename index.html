Hi There!

My name is Hamza Ruzayqat and I am currently a Post-Doctoral Research Fellow at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology in the group of Computational Probability (COMPPROB) led by Prof. Ajay Jasra. In May 2019, I obtained my PhD and Concurrent Masters degrees in Mathematics from the University of Tennessee, Knoxville, TN, USA. In 2012, I earned my Bachelor degree in Physics and Mathematics from Birzeit University, Birzeit, Palestine.

I am also a husband and a father!

Research
